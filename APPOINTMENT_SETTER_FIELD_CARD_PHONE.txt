SOLAR APPOINTMENT SETTER FIELD CARD (PHONE FILE)

Use this in the field. Keep it simple, fast, and repeatable.

==================================================
1) 10-SECOND MENTAL RESET (BETWEEN DOORS)
==================================================
"Fresh door, fresh start. Smile, posture, pace. Ask one good question and go for the appointment."

==================================================
2) DOORSTEP SCRIPT (CORE)
==================================================
"Hey, I'm [Name] with [Company] - I'll be quick.
We're helping homeowners on this street review utility costs and solar eligibility before rates change.
Quick question: are you the homeowner?
Perfect. We do a short 10-15 minute savings and eligibility review.
Would today at [Time 1] or [Time 2] work better?"

SCRIPT RULES
- Keep it under 20-30 seconds
- Ask questions early
- Offer two specific times (never "what works?")
- Stay calm, smile, and pause

==================================================
3) QUALIFICATION (S.O.L.A.R.)
==================================================
- S - Homeowner status confirmed
- O - Ownership timeline (ideally staying 2+ years)
- L - Meaningful electric bill / usage
- A - All decision-makers can attend
- R - Roof/property likely fit (or open to alternatives)

FAST QUALIFIER LINE
"So I don't waste your time, you own the home and both decision-makers can be there, right?"

==================================================
4) OBJECTION CHEAT SHEET (LACE)
==================================================
Listen -> Acknowledge -> Clarify -> Engage + Re-ask
Keep rebuttals under 20 seconds.

"NOT INTERESTED."
"Totally fair - most people say that before seeing real numbers.
Is it solar in general, or just bad timing?"

RE-ASK
"Makes sense. Let's do a quick 15-minute review so you can decide with data.
Is [Time 1] or [Time 2] better?"

"I'M BUSY."
"I hear you. That's why it's only 10-15 minutes.
Better later today or tomorrow?"

"NEED TO TALK TO MY SPOUSE."
"Absolutely. Let's set one time when both of you are there so you only do this once."

"JUST LEAVE INFO."
"Happy to. The numbers only make sense with your actual usage, so let's book 15 minutes and I'll leave a summary after."

==================================================
5) APPOINTMENT LOCK-IN (SHOW RATE)
==================================================
1. Confirm exact time + address
2. Confirm all decision-makers attending
3. Confirm best phone number
4. Set expectation: "10-15 minutes"
5. Send confirmation text immediately

LOCK-IN LINE
"Perfect, you're set for [Day/Time] at [Address], with [Other Decision-Maker] joining.
This is a quick 10-15 minute review. Best number for confirmation?"

CONFIRMATION TEXT
"Hi [Name], this is [Rep] with [Company]. Confirming your solar review at [Time] at [Address].
We'll keep it to ~15 min. Please have all decision-makers present. Reply YES to confirm."

==================================================
6) REJECTION RECOVERY (UNDER 20 SECONDS)
==================================================
1. Exhale, shoulders down
2. Quick lesson: opener / qualifier / ask?
3. Next door immediately

No emotional carryover.

==================================================
7) DAILY NON-NEGOTIABLES
==================================================
- 80-120 doors
- 20-35 homeowner conversations
- 8-15 full pitches
- 2-5 qualified appointments set
- 30 minutes of roleplay
- Follow-up block every evening

==================================================
8) END-OF-DAY FOLLOW-UP
==================================================
- Immediate confirmation when set
- Morning reminder on appointment day
- 30-60 minute reminder before appointment
- No-show: reschedule within 10 minutes

NO-SHOW TEXT
"Hey [Name], we missed you today - no problem.
Want to rebook a quick 15-minute review for [Option 1] or [Option 2]?"

==================================================
9) PERFORMANCE STANDARD
==================================================
You are paid for disciplined execution.

Track the numbers daily. Improve the weakest conversion step first:
Knocks -> Conversations -> Pitches -> Sets -> Shows
